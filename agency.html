About

The Agency

Model agency and management. We discover, develop, and represent extraordinary talent for fashion, media, and advertising.

Who We Are

Raw Division is an international model management agency headquartered in Berlin with a global network spanning New York, Paris, Milan, London, and beyond. We work with the world's leading brands, photographers, and creative directors.

Founded on the principle that authentic beauty commands attention, we represent women, men, new faces, and curved talent across every dimension of the fashion and commercial industry.

Our Approach

We work closely with each client from initial casting and sedcard selection through to travel management and final production. Our bookers ensure every model arrives prepared, professional, and on time -- so you can focus entirely on your creative vision.

With over 20 years of combined experience, our team understands the nuances of international bookings, editorial requirements, and commercial production at every scale.

What We Do

01

Model Booking

Photo and video production for advertising, e-commerce, editorial, and campaigns. Personalized sedcard selections tailored to your brief.

02

Fashion Shows

Runway talent for fashion weeks, showrooms, presentations, and brand events. From fittings to the final walk.

03

Talent Scouting

We discover and develop the next generation of talent through international scouting, open castings, and digital submissions.

04

E-Commerce

High-volume lookbook and online store productions with reliable, experienced models who deliver consistent results.

05

Campaign & Advertising

Full-service model placement for national and international advertising campaigns. Rights management and usage licensing included.

06

International Management

Placement and coordination across international markets. Travel planning, visa support, and mother agency partnerships worldwide.
