About

The Agency

We connect remarkable people for dining, events, travel and lasting friendships. This is cultural exchange done right.

Who We Are

Raw Division is a cultural exchange and companionship agency headquartered in London with a global network spanning Thessaloniki, Dubai and beyond. We connect people who value great conversation, shared experiences and meaningful connections.

Founded on the principle that the best experiences are shared ones, we represent a diverse network of companions for dining, events, travel and social occasions.

Our Approach

We work closely with each client from the first conversation through to the event itself. Our team ensures every companion is prepared, punctual and perfectly matched to your occasion.

With over 20 years of combined experience, our team understands the nuances of social dynamics, cultural etiquette and what makes a great pairing.

Our Services

01

Dining Companions

02

Event Partners

Social companions for galas, premieres, corporate functions and cultural events. Confident, well-presented and ready for any occasion.
